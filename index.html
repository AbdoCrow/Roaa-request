About Us

How did the company Start

One day, I was running home. Suddenly, I found a weird lizard! I hate lizards! Anyway, a big dog came and gulp ate the whole lizard. That made me think, "Wow, cheesecakes are really good. Why not open a bookstore?" And that's how it all began!

Key Features

  • A distinct lack of dragons: We're proud to offer a dragon-free environment, perfect for those who prefer their literary adventures to remain strictly fictional.
  • An abundance of oxygen: Breathe easy! We maintain a carefully curated atmosphere with GOOD oxygen for your browsing pleasure.
  • Gravity: Books stay put on the shelves (mostly). We've harnessed the power of gravity to prevent unexpected book avalanches.
  • Mostly quiet (sometimes): While we can't guarantee absolute silence (squirrels are our librarians, after all), we strive to create a generally peaceful atmosphere for reading "If you can!".
